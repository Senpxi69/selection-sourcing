Who We Are

We're a specialist HR sourcing and recruitment partner, helping companies across India find the right people and helping professionals find the right roles.

Founded in 2014, Selections Sourcing was built on a simple (and slightly stubborn) conviction: recruitment is about people before positions. Every hiring decision carries weight, it shapes a business, influences a team, and changes the course of someone's career.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted recruitment partner serving organizations across India, with expertise spanning IT and Non-IT hiring. We work with businesses of every size to find professionals who don't just tick boxes on a JD, they actually fit the culture, values, and vision.
